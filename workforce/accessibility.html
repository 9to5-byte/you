Accessibility Statement

Last updated: February 2026

Workforce Pty Ltd is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Our Commitment

We believe that everyone should be able to access information about our labour hire services. We strive to make our website accessible to all users, regardless of ability or technology used to access it.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

Navigation

  • Skip to main content link for keyboard users
  • Consistent navigation throughout the site
  • Descriptive link text
  • Keyboard-accessible menus and forms

Visual Design

  • Sufficient colour contrast for text readability
  • Resizable text without loss of functionality
  • Clear visual hierarchy with proper heading structure
  • Focus indicators for interactive elements

Content

  • Alternative text for images
  • Descriptive labels for form fields
  • Clear and simple language
  • Logical content structure

Technical

  • Valid HTML markup
  • ARIA landmarks and labels where appropriate
  • Responsive design for various devices and screen sizes
  • Compatible with common assistive technologies

Standards

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities.

Assistive Technology Compatibility

Our website is designed to be compatible with:

  • Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation

Known Limitations

While we strive to ensure accessibility, some content may have limitations:

  • Some older PDF documents may not be fully accessible
  • Third-party embedded content (such as maps) may have limited accessibility
  • Some images may have limited descriptions

We are actively working to address these issues.

Continuous Improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review our website and make improvements to ensure we maintain and enhance accessibility. Our team receives training on accessibility best practices.

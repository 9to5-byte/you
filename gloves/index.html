About Us

Estonia's Trusted Workwear Specialists Since 2012

ProGrip was founded with a simple mission: to provide Estonian workers and professionals with high-quality, durable work gloves that combine safety with comfort. What started as a small supply store has grown into one of Estonia's most trusted workwear suppliers.

We work directly with certified manufacturers to source premium materials, and our expert team ensures every glove meets strict safety standards. Whether you're a construction worker, mechanic, gardener, or DIY enthusiast, we have the right glove solution for you.

About Us

Estonia's Trusted Gate Specialists Since 2010

Liugvärav was founded with a simple mission: to provide Estonian homeowners and property managers with high-quality, durable garden gates that combine security with aesthetic appeal. What started as a small family workshop has grown into one of Estonia's most trusted gate suppliers.

We work directly with European manufacturers to source premium materials, and our skilled installation team ensures every gate is fitted perfectly. Whether you own a private house in Tallinn, a summer cottage in the countryside, or manage an apartment complex, we have the right gate solution for you.
